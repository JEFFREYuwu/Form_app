                                          PROJECT

THE APP IS A SIMPLE APPLICATION FORM THAT FINDS THE SUITABILITY OF AN APPLICANTS FOR A TRAINER ROLE.


                                          OUTCOME

AFTER SUCCESSFULLY PASSING THE APPLICANTION FORM, THE APPLICANT'S CV IS EMAILED TO HR.

ON FAILING TO PASS THE APPLICANTION FORM, THE APPLICANT IS INFORMED OF THEIR UNSUCCESSFUL APLLICATION.
